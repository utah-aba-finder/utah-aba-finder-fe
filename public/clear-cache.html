Clear Browser Cache

If you're experiencing certificate issues on mobile browsers, try clearing your browser cache:

For Mobile Browsers:

  • Safari (iOS): Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data
  • Chrome (Android): Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear browsing data
  • Firefox (Mobile): Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear private data

For Desktop Browsers:

  • Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  • Safari: Develop > Empty Caches (enable Develop menu in Preferences > Advanced)
